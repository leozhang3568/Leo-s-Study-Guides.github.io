# Clothing

&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Romani dress is the traditional clothing worn by the Romani people. Although differing by subgroup, Romani clothing still shares some similarities regardless of the Romani group it originates from. Some Romani wished to show their abundance and prosperity through their traditional clothing. Romani clothing for women is based upon the ideas of purity and the Romani ideals of being “unpolluted.” It is also influenced by the idea that showing your wealth is a way of good fortune.
