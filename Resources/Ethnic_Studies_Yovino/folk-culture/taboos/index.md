# Taboos

	Over many generations, they have gradually built These are some actions that a Romani Gypsy can’t do:

- Walk barefoot (superstition) 

- Put a pair of shoes on a table (superstition)

- Wear new shoes when pregnant (superstition)

- Walking over a man’s food (for females, superstition)

- Cut someone’s hair when they are younger than two years old (superstition)

- Burn bread or flour (religion)

- Not apologizing to bread right after they dropped it (religion)

- Stepping on bread (religion)
