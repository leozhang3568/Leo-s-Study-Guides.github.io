# Food

&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; nbsp; Some Romani Gypsies, even today, live in poverty, so naturally, they cherish their food. Some of them even forage for food in order to glean their nutrition. Similar to their religion and music, Romani cuisine carries a hint of the place that they are served in. Religiously, bread is one of their most sacred foods, and they have invented many recipies for it. One dish, called lemon bread, has a sweet and pungent taste that makes a nice breakfast dish for Romani Gypsies. Some other traditional foods include staples like *blinis* (crepes). Traditional food is significant to Romani culture because it is used to inform people about the culture. Also, by opening restaurants, Romani people are showing that they care about serving people with their cultural food. Romani food strives in today’s world of discrimmination towards them and are used as a way to showcase their culture.

## Recipe for Traditional Romani Lemon Bread

![](https://therecipecritic.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/lemonbreadhero.jpg)

### Ingredients

“1 2/3 cups all purpose flour  

1 teaspoon baking powder 

1/2 teaspoon salt 

1 1/2 cups sugar  

1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, room temperature  

2 large eggs  

2 teaspoons (packed) grated lemon peel  

1/2 cup whole milk  

1/4 cup fresh lemon juice”<sup><a href="#id-3">3</a></sup>

### Procedure

1. Preheat conventional oven at 350&deg; F.  

2. Lightly butter loaf pan.  

3. Mix flour, salt, and baking powder in a medium bowl.  

4. Use electric mixer to beat 1 cup butter and sugar, and stop when fluffy and light.  

5. Beat in eggs one at a time.  

6. Mix in grated lemon peel.  

7. Mix in all dry ingredients.  

8. Pour batter into buttered loaf pan.  

9. Bake until tester comes out clean (takes about an hour).  

10. While baking, mix 1/2 cup sugar and lemon juice in saucepan and stir in low heat before mixure becomes a solution.  

11. When done baking, put bread on rack. Pour lemon gaze onto bread as it is absorbed. Let stand at room temperature. Serve. Enjoy!
