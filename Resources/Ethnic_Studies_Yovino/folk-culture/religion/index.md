# Religion/belief system

&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Similar to music, Romani Gypsies tend to be influenced by their own countries’ religions and belief systems after they have immigrated to them. They do not have a single, traditional faith but multiple, diverse religions, including Catholism and Baptist Roma. Romani Gypsies have different practices regarding birth, marriages, and death, but they tend to follow specific patterns. They believe that they should always be *Rronamo*, meaning that they need to behave with respect and dignity.
