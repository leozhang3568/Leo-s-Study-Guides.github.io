# Music

&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Romani music, known to outsiders as Gypsy music, is very culturally significant to Romani Gypsies. Romani music have traveled a great significance, embodied by their faithful players.

## Romani Folk Music

&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Romani folk music is a style of Gypsy music based on Romani musical traditions. They lack any relationship with the traditions of the place where they are played in. The music mainly contains singing, and fast sections of music are accompanied b dancing, tongue-clacking, hand clapping, and other sound-making. Romani folk music always starts after a beat, known as synchopation. They also contain three vocal parts with emphasis on rhythm.

## Romani Country Music

&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Since Romani Gypsies have migrated to Europe and other places, their music have been influenced by the music heard around them. Local music is slowly transformed into Romani styles, which are often more sophisticated than the original local music.
