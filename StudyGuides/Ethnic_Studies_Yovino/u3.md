# Unit 3 Study Guide

Question | Answer
--- | ---
What is a Gringo  | 	Term used to refer to English-speaking Americans
What is a barrio  | 	Neighborhood or district, often associated with a particular ethnic group
What is repatriation  | 	Returning to one's original home
Mexican laborers mostly worked in which sector  | 	Agriculture
What was the point of the Mexican Revolution in 1910  | 	land reform
What is a PUSH factor  | 	Gang violence
What is a pull factor  | 	Economic opportunities
Where are Sikhs mostly from | 	Punjab
When was the Great Depression  | 	1930's
Term for something that is dirty and unkempt | 	Squalid
What is El Norte  | 	Region of the US located north of the border with Mexico
The right to vote in political elections | Suffrage
How were Mexicans displaced after Mexico lost the war to the USA  | 	Their property was seized by the Anglo government
What is a stereotype | 	a widely held but fixed and oversimplified image or idea of a particular type of person or thing.
What did the Treaty of Guadalupe Hidalgo give the USA  | 	vast territories from Mexico
What document ended the Mexican American War  | 	Treaty of Guadalupe Hidalgo
What is Manifest Destiny  | 	The idea that the US is destined by God to expand it's territory
What does the term "squatter" refer to  | 	A person who settles on land without legal claim or right
Who is a "descendant"  | 	A person who is descended from a particular ancestor
Which term describes the process of granting citizenship of a country through legal procedures  | 	Naturalized
Who are referred to as "Anglos"  | 	People of English-speaking heritage
What does the term "mongrel refer to  | 	A person of mixed race or ancestry
Which term is used to describe a society or culture that is characterized by advanced social development and cultural refinement  | 	Civilized
What term refers to the act of extending or enlarging a territory, typically through colonization or annexation  | 	Expansion
